About Us

First Last

TD Impex, established in 2013, has been actively trading with Western Africa and gradually increasing its reach in other parts of the world. Being a plastics manufacturer allows us to give our customers maximum variety and flexibility for our products. Other products are outsourced from India are sent all across the globe. Quality and Reaching the customers’ demands via full customizability and flexibility are the top priorities for TD Impex. Along with these qualities, honesty and integrity are fundamental values that give customers the highest confidence in the company, allowing them always to trust and depend on TD Impex.
